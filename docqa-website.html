Process
From document to answer in four steps

A production-grade RAG pipeline runs behind every query — invisible to you, precise in every result.

Step 01

Upload your documents

Drag in PDFs, Word documents, or plain text. Batch uploads supported. Files stay private and encrypted at rest.

Step 02

Intelligent chunking

Text is split into overlapping 512-token chunks with metadata — filename, page number, section heading.

Step 03

Semantic retrieval

Your question is embedded and matched against stored vectors using cosine similarity with MMR diversity ranking.

Step 04

Grounded answer generation

GPT-4o synthesises a precise answer using only retrieved context — with source citations for every claim.
